Structure Attribute​Decodable 

public struct AttributeDecodable: Decodable

A type-erased Decodable value.

The AttributeDecodable type forwards decoding responsibilities to an underlying value, hiding its specific underlying type.

You can decode mixed-type values in dictionaries and other collections that require Decodable conformance by declaring their contained type to be AttributeDecodable.
