Structure Attribute 

public struct Attribute: Codable

A type-erased Codable value.

The Attribute type forwards encoding and decoding responsibilities to an underlying value, hiding its specific underlying type.

You can encode or decode mixed-type values in dictionaries and other collections that require Encodable or Decodable conformance by declaring their contained type to be Attribute.
